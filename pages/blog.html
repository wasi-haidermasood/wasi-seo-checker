How to fix the top 10 on-page SEO issues (free checklist)

Published: Aug 27, 2025

Use this checklist to fix common on-page problems quickly — perfect for ecommerce and small websites.

Checklist

  1. Improve title tags to 50–60 chars
  2. Write helpful meta descriptions (120–160 chars)
  3. Ensure single H1 per page
  4. Add descriptive alt text to images
  5. Fix broken links
